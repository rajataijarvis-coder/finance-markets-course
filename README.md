# 📚 Financial Markets: Zero to Hero

A comprehensive, beginner-friendly course on financial markets for non-finance professionals.

**Target Audience:** Complete beginners with zero finance background  
**Learning Style:** Bite-sized modules, practical examples, no jargon  
**Duration:** ~40 hours over 2-3 weeks  
**Delivery:** Every few hours via WhatsApp/Signal with interactive Q&A

---

## 🎯 Course Objectives

By the end of this course, you will:
- ✅ Understand how money flows through the global financial system
- ✅ Know the difference between equities, bonds, derivatives, and currencies
- ✅ Understand what central banks do and why they matter
- ✅ Read financial news with confidence
- ✅ Evaluate investment opportunities using basic frameworks
- ✅ Understand risk, return, and portfolio basics

---

## 📋 Curriculum Overview

### Phase 1: Foundations (Modules 1-5)
1. **What is Money?** — From barter to digital currencies
2. **The Financial System Map** — Banks, markets, and intermediaries
3. **Interest Rates Explained** — Time value of money
4. **Risk & Return Basics** — The fundamental trade-off
5. **Market Participants** — Who buys, who sells, and why

### Phase 2: Core Markets (Modules 6-15)
6. **Equity Markets (Stocks)** — Ownership, dividends, price discovery
7. **Fixed Income (Bonds)** — Lending to governments & companies
8. **Money Markets** — Short-term cash instruments
9. **Foreign Exchange (Forex)** — Currencies and exchange rates
10. **Commodities** — Physical goods as investments
11. **Derivatives: Futures & Forwards** — Agreements to trade later
12. **Derivatives: Options** — The right (but not obligation) to trade
13. **Derivatives: Swaps** — Exchanging cash flows
14. **Credit Markets** — Loans, lending, and credit risk
15. **Alternative Investments** — Real estate, private equity, hedge funds

### Phase 3: Market Infrastructure (Modules 16-22)
16. **Stock Exchanges** — How markets actually work
17. **Clearing & Settlement** — Making trades official
18. **Market Makers & Liquidity** — How prices stay fair
19. **Trading Mechanisms** — Orders, matching, and algorithms
20. **Market Regulation** — Keeping markets honest
21. **Market Data** — Prices, indices, and information
22. **Electronic Trading** — How technology changed everything

### Phase 4: Central Banking & Policy (Modules 23-28)
23. **What Central Banks Do** — The role of the Fed, ECB, BoE
24. **Monetary Policy Tools** — Interest rates, QE, open market ops
25. **Inflation Targeting** — Why 2% matters
26. **Currency Policy** — Managing exchange rates
27. **Financial Stability** — Preventing crashes
28. **Crisis Response** — 2008, COVID, and lessons learned

### Phase 5: Practical Applications (Modules 29-35)
29. **Reading Financial News** — Bloomberg, Reuters, FT decoder
30. **Economic Indicators** — GDP, unemployment, PMI explained
31. **Company Analysis Basics** — Reading financial statements
32. **Valuation Fundamentals** — Is something worth the price?
33. **Portfolio Theory Basics** — Diversification explained
34. **Behavioral Finance** — Why humans are bad at investing
35. **Fintech & Crypto** — New frontiers in finance
