## 🤔 What is Price Action?

Price action = trading without indicators.

Instead of RSI, MACD, or moving averages, you read the raw price bars to make decisions.

**The philosophy:**
- Indicators lag (they're derived from price)
- Price is the ultimate truth
- Learn to read the market's footprint

In this module, we'll cover:
- Reading price action
- Advanced support/resistance concepts
- Supply and demand zones
- Trading price action setups

---

## 📖 The Building Blocks of Price Action

### The Single Bar

Every bar tells a story:

```
Bullish Bar:
- Close higher than open
- Buyers won the battle
- Higher close = more bullish

Bearish Bar:
- Close lower than open  
- Sellers won the battle
- Lower close = more bearish
```

### Bar Characteristics

| Feature | What it tells you |
|---------|-------------------|
| Body size | Strength of move |
| Wick size | Rejection of prices |
| Position | Who's in control |
| Volume | Conviction |

### Reading Multiple Bars

**Trend bars:**
```
Uptrend:    Large green bars, small wicks
Downtrend:  Large red bars, small wicks
```

**Reversal bars:**
```
Shooting star: Small body, long upper wick
Hammer:       Small body, long lower wick
```

## 🔑 Advanced Support & Resistance

### What Makes a Level Strong?

Levels become stronger when:

1. **Tested multiple times** — More tests = stronger level
2. **Recent vs. old** — Recent levels matter more
3. **Volume at level** — High volume = strong interest
4. **Psychological levels** — Round numbers (100, 200, 1000)

### Types of Support/Resistance

**1. Horizontal levels:**
```
Price
  │
200 │─────────────
  │          ╱──╲
150 │────────╱    ╲───
  │        ╱
100 │───────╱
     Time
```

**2. Trendline support/resistance:**
```
Price
  │
200 │          ╱
  │         ╱
150 │        ╱─── Trendline
  │       ╱
100 │──────╱
     Time
```

**3. Moving average as S/R:**
```
Price
  │
200 │─────────────── SMA 200
  │          ╱────╲
150 │────────╱      ╲─────────
  │        ╱
100 │───────╱
     Time
```

**4. Round numbers:**
```
$50, $100, $150, $200...
1000, 2000, 3000... (S&P 500)
```

### The Flip

When support breaks, it becomes resistance. When resistance breaks, it becomes support.

```
Before:                    After breakout:
$100 (resistance)          $100 now support
    ╱                            |
   ╱                            |
  ╱──────────                   +--------- Breakout
 /                           /
/                           /
|                           |
```

## 📊 Supply & Demand Zones

### The Concept

Instead of single lines, think in **zones** where institutions buy or sell.

**Demand Zone (Buying):**
```
Zone: $90-$100
- Where buyers stepped in before
- Place buy orders in the zone
- Stop below zone
```

**Supply Zone (Selling):**
```
Zone: $150-$160
- Where sellers stepped in before
- Place sell orders in the zone
- Stop above zone
```

### Identifying Zones

**Steps:**
1. Look for strong reversal bars (large bodies, small wicks)
2. Mark the base of the move (where impulse started)
3. That's your zone

**Example:**
```
Stock falls from $150 to $100
At $100: large green bar with small wick, huge volume
→ Demand zone: $100-$105

Stock rallies to $140
At $140: large red bar with small wick, huge volume  
→ Supply zone: $135-$140
```

## 🎯 Price Action Setups

### Setup 1: Bounce off Support

**Entry:** Buy when price bounces from support  
**Stop:** Below support  
**Target:** Previous high or next resistance

```
        ╱──────────────────
       ╱ 
      ╱
     ╱ ← Bounce here
────╱
  ↑
Support
```

**Confirmation:**
- Bullish reversal bar at support
- Bullish candle pattern (engulfing, hammer)
- Volume increase

### Setup 2: Breakout

**Entry:** Buy when price breaks resistance  
**Stop:** Below breakout level  
**Target:** Measure move (same distance as prior swing)

```
    ╱──────────
   ╱  Breakout!
  ╱──────────
─╱───────────────
```

**Confirmation:**
- Strong close above level (not wick)
- Volume increase on breakout
- Retest of level (optional)

### Setup 3: Trend Continuation

**Entry:** Buy on pullbacks to moving average  
**Stop:** Below moving average  
**Target:** New high

```
         ╱───────────────
        ╱
       ╱
──────╱ ← Pullback to SMA 50
      │
      SMA 50
```

**Best used in:** Strong trends

### Setup 4: Double Bottom (W-bottom)

**Entry:** Buy at second bottom  
**Stop:** Below lows  
**Target:** Neckline breakout

```
         ╲    B₂
        ╲  ╱  ╲
       ╲  ╱    ╲───────
      ╲  ╱      ╲
──────╲ ╱        ╲───
  B₁  ╲╱          ╲
      Trough
```

**Confirmation:**
- Second bottom holds
- Bullish momentum (RSI, MACD)
- Volume on breakout

## 📈 Reading Volume

### Volume Basics

Volume = number of contracts traded

**High volume:** Strong conviction
**Low volume:** Weak move (could reverse)

### Volume Patterns

**1. High volume at breakout:**
```
Good! Institutions are participating
```

**2. Low volume at breakout:**
```
Bad! Likely false breakout
```

**3. High volume at reversal:**
```
Good! Institutions are taking the other side
```

**4. Volume declining in trend:**
```
Warning! Trend losing steam
```

### Volume + Price Action

```
Price: Higher highs, higher lows
Volume: Increasing on rallies, decreasing on pullbacks
→ Healthy uptrend
```

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

### 1. Drawing Too Many Levels
Don't draw every line you see. Only key levels.

### 2. Forgetting the Trend
Don't buy support in a downtrend unless you're trading a reversal.

### 3. Not Waiting for Confirmation
Don't guess the bounce. Wait for the bar to close.

### 4. Placing Stops Too Tight
Markets often wick to liquidity pools. Give room.

### 5. Ignoring the Bigger Picture
Check higher timeframes before trading.

## 🔑 Key Takeaways

1. **Price action:**
   - Trade based on price bars, not indicators
   - Body = close vs. open
   - Wicks = rejection of prices

2. **Support/Resistance:**
   - Levels tested more = stronger
   - Recent > old
   - Round numbers matter

3. **Supply/Demand zones:**
   - Think in zones, not lines
   - Base of moves = key zones
   - Institutional activity = high volume

4. **Key setups:**
   - Bounce off support
   - Breakout of resistance
   - Trend continuation
   - Double bottom/top

5. **Volume:**
   - Confirms moves
   - High = conviction
   - Low = weak
